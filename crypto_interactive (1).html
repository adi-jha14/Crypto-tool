Hill Cipher — 2×2

Enter your plaintext and 2×2 key matrix. The tool will encrypt and show the full step-by-step workings including decryption key.

ℹ  Key matrix must be invertible mod 26 — i.e. det(K) must be coprime with 26. Plaintext will be padded with X if length is odd.

Rail Fence Cipher

Enter your plaintext and number of rails. The zigzag pattern and each rail's characters will be shown visually.

ℹ  Minimum 2 rails. Non-alpha characters are stripped. Uppercase conversion is automatic. Use Decrypt mode to reverse a Rail Fence ciphertext.

RSA Encryption

Enter two primes p and q, a public exponent e, and your message M. The tool computes n, φ(n), d, encrypts and decrypts with full steps.

ℹ  p and q must be prime. e must satisfy 1 < e < φ(n) and gcd(e, φ(n)) = 1. Enter a character/text to auto-fill M, or type M directly. Each character is encrypted separately when text is entered.
